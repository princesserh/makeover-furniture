About us

At Makeover Furniture we are passionate about three things: People, Planet and Community

We change lives for the better in practical, measurable and impactful ways through work experience, traineeships and business incubation. Giving people a chance to become their best selves through employment training. You can make a difference to many individuals, the wider community and the planet by supporting our work. Our work experience trainees bring new life to salvaged, vintage and antique items to create beautiful and affordable bespoke furniture. Our business incubation trainees have beautiful, artisan goods available in our high street shop. As a charitable social enterprise what goes in gets reinvested directly back into our people, programmes and the community.
